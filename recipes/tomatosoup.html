Tomato soup

Description

Some good old tomato soup

Incredients

  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Vegetable stock
  • Salt and other spices

Steps

  1. Fry the onions
  2. Add tomatoes, I don't recommend to use the liquid in the can, but it's up to You
  3. Add the stock
  4. Add all the spices and let it boil atleast 15 minutes
