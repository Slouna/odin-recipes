Ingredients

Ragú (Red sauce)

  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Chili
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Celery
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 70g Bacon
  • 400g Ground beef
  • 70g tomato puree
  • 2dl Red wine
  • 5dl Broth

White sauce

  • 1l Milk
  • 50g Butter
  • 2dl Flour
  • 100g Parmesan
  • Touch of nutmeg

And...

  • Lasagna plates
  • 100g Parmesan
  • Basil
  • Butter

Steps

Ragú

  1. Make your broth
  2. Chop the vegetables, and put them in to the pan in mild heat.
  3. Add chopped bacon and let the greace come out of it.
  4. Add grounded beef, and let it brown.
  5. Add red wine and let it evaporate.
  6. Add tomato puree and fry it a little.
  7. Add about half of your broth and let it boil slowly.
  8. Add rest of your broth slowly if the ragú looks dry.
  9. Let the ragú brew atleast 1,5h.

White sauce

  1. Put the butter in the kettle.
  2. Add flour and let it brown just a little bit.
  3. Add milk slowly. Be careful not to burn it.
  4. Finally add parmesan and nutmeg

Building lasagna

  1. Put Ragu at the bottom of tin
  2. Add little parmesan on top of it
  3. Put lasagna plate
  4. Put some white sauce next
  5. Again add parmesan and lasagna plate
  6. Continue this until all sauces are used, but I don't recomend more than six layers
  7. Last layer should be white sauce
  8. On top of it add some butter, rest of the parmesan, and Some basil leaves
  9. Put in the oven for 45 minutes or as long as lasagne plates are adviced to be in the oven
  10. Enjoy
