Risotto

Very awesome risotto recipe

Incrediendts

  • Shallot
  • Risotto rice
  • White wine
  • Good broth
  • Butter
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Fry the shallots in the butter
  2. Add the rice with the shallots
  3. Add the white wine
  4. Add some of the broth
  5. Let it boil slowly, don't let it dry, but don't let it drown either, add more broth when needed
  6. This should take 20-30 minutes, depending how you like it
  7. Add loads of parmesan and mix it well
  8. Enjoy!
